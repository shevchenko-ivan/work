How I work

Three things I bring to every SaaS engagement.

01

Multi-role SaaS thinking

I treat dashboards as workflows, not screens. Roles, permissions, edge states and data density are designed first — pixels follow.

02

Design systems that actually ship

Tokens, Storybook, governance and contribution flows — built so engineers can ship faster, not so designers can admire Figma libraries.

03

Metrics-driven prioritisation

RICE, heuristics and stakeholder interviews drive scope. I will tell you what to cut, not only what to design.
