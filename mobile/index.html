How I approach mobile

Three principles I bring to every mobile project.

01

Respect the platform

Native gestures, native pickers, native haptics. The best “custom” solution is usually the one that follows iOS HIG or Material — and knows when to break the rule on purpose.

02

Cross-platform fluency

I’ve shipped on Flutter and worked with React Native teams. I think in shared patterns first and platform-specific deltas second.

03

Accessibility as a default

WCAG 2.1 AA isn’t a final-stage checklist for me — it’s baked into colour tokens, touch targets and motion specs from the first component.
